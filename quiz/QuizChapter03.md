# Chapter 3 Quiz: Cancer Epidemiology and Risk Factors

## Instructions
Choose the best answer for each question. Each question tests understanding of key concepts from cancer epidemiology and risk factors.

---

**Question 1:** According to GLOBOCAN 2022 estimates, approximately how many new cancer cases and deaths occurred worldwide in 2022?

a) 10 million new cases and 5 million deaths

b) 15 million new cases and 7 million deaths

c) 20 million new cases and 9.7 million deaths

d) 25 million new cases and 12 million deaths

---

**Question 2:** Which cancer type is both the most commonly diagnosed cancer AND the leading cause of cancer death globally in both sexes combined?

a) Breast cancer

b) Colorectal cancer

c) Lung cancer

d) Prostate cancer

---

**Question 3:** What percentage of the global population resides in Asia, and what proportion of global cancer cases occurred there in 2022?

a) 45% of population; 35% of cases

b) 50% of population; 45% of cases

c) 59% of population; 49% of cases

d) 65% of population; 60% of cases

---

**Question 4:** What percentage of cancers are estimated to be directly attributable to environmental exposures rather than genetic factors?

a) 30-40%

b) 50-60%

c) 70-90%

d) Nearly 100%

---

**Question 5:** Smoking accounts for approximately what percentage of all cancer deaths?

a) At least 10%

b) At least 20%

c) At least 30%

d) At least 50%

---

**Question 6:** How many chemicals are present in combustible tobacco cigarette smoke, and how many are established human carcinogens?

a) More than 1,000 chemicals; at least 20 carcinogens

b) More than 3,000 chemicals; at least 40 carcinogens

c) More than 7,000 chemicals; at least 60-70 carcinogens

d) More than 10,000 chemicals; at least 100 carcinogens

---

**Question 7:** Which of the following cancers have increased risk associated with obesity and being overweight?

a) Only breast and colorectal cancer

b) Breast, endometrial, colorectal, kidney, and esophageal cancer

c) Only lung and liver cancer

d) Only stomach and pancreatic cancer

---

**Question 8:** According to the International Agency for Research on Cancer (IARC), approximately how many known or suspected carcinogens had been identified as of 2007?

a) 50

b) 150

c) 415

d) 1,000

---

**Question 9:** What percentage of cancers worldwide does the World Health Organization (WHO) estimate are attributable to environmental risks?

a) 5%

b) 10%

c) 20%

d) 40%

---

**Question 10:** Which racial/ethnic group in the United States has the highest death rates for many cancer types, despite having slightly lower incidence rates for some cancers like breast cancer?

a) Hispanic/Latino populations

b) Asian American populations

c) Black/African American populations

d) Native American populations

---

## Answer Key

1. **c)** 20 million new cases and 9.7 million deaths

2. **c)** Lung cancer

3. **c)** 59% of population; 49% of cases

4. **c)** 70-90%

5. **c)** At least 30%

6. **c)** More than 7,000 chemicals; at least 60-70 carcinogens

7. **b)** Breast, endometrial, colorectal, kidney, and esophageal cancer

8. **c)** 415

9. **c)** 20%

10. **c)** Black/African American populations
